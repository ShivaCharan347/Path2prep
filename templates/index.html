Path2Prep - Study Plan

Path2Prep offers a comprehensive, AI-driven study plan designed to guide students through a structured and efficient learning journey tailored to their individual goals and progress. Targeted toward students preparing for competitive exams, Path2Prep uses generative AI to create a personalized curriculum, breaking down complex topics into manageable milestones and weekly objectives. By analyzing student performance data, the AI system identifies strengths and weaknesses, generating dynamic content such as quizzes, summaries, and revision guides. This adaptive approach enables timely interventions, helping students reinforce weak areas and stay on track with their study schedules. With its emphasis on personalized pacing and real-time progress tracking, Path2Prep empowers students to achieve academic success with greater focus and confidence.
